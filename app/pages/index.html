About Qkids

Qkids is an online teaching community where thousands of people from the USA and Canada teach kids English online.

We are seeking college students, school teachers, stay-at-home moms, and all those who have a passion for education to work part-time (minimum of 6 hours per week).

Knowledge of Chinese is not necessary. Each class is 30 minutes. All curricula are preset and incorporated with animated characters and games. There is paid training before orientation. Further training and support is provided along with coursework.
